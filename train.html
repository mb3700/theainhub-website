Why training

A workshop opens the door. Training walks through it.

Most teams try AI, get a couple of wins, and then stall, because no one taught them how to build something that lasts. Training is where a team stops depending on one power user and starts running with AI as a habit.

What we teach

The skills that make it stick.

Prompting that holds up

How to ask so you get the same good result every time. Reliable, repeatable, and tailored to your work.

Build skills & CLAUDE.md files

Package how your team actually works into reusable skills and the CLAUDE.md files the tools follow, so AI does it your way.

Capture & automate a workflow

Take one real, repetitive workflow and automate it end to end, with a human in the loop at every step.

Know what to trust

Experiment, test, and judge the output, so your team knows when to rely on AI and when to double‑check it.

How it runs

Multi‑session, hands‑on, on your real work.

This isn’t a one‑off seminar. We work with the same small group over several sessions, building on real tasks from your business, so the skills land where the work actually happens.

Mark oversees every engagement; our students work right alongside your team, bringing current AI fluency while your people bring the domain expertise. It goes both ways.

  • Several sessions, so the skills build over time
  • Your real workflows as the material
  • A small group to start. Then we keep going, group after group
  • Students embedded alongside, faculty‑overseen
  • You keep everything you build: skills, files, automations
