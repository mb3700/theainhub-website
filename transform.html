What it is

Not a strategy deck. The thing itself.

Integration is where AI stops being a tool a few people poke at and becomes part of how the work gets done. We don’t hand you a roadmap and a barrier map—we build a working automation, show the hours it gives back, and leave your team able to run it.

How we do it

Capture. Build. Prove. Spread.

Four steps, weeks not years, with a human in the loop the whole way.

1

Capture

We record an expert doing the real work (30–60 min) and turn it into a knowledge base and a reusable skill.

2

Build

We build it into Claude—and into internal apps with Replit and Vercel—then test, refine, and automate.

3

Prove

A working automation and hours back, in weeks. Not a roadmap, not a deck—the thing itself.

4

Spread

The trained 3–5 become champions who carry it to the next team. The capability stays in‑house.

Proof · Storm Smart

An hour a day, back—times 33 reps.

A storm‑protection manufacturer that doubled its headcount. We started with sales—33 reps—and recorded the best rep’s hour of pre‑consult prep.

Before
The best rep’s prep lived in one head. Every rep spent ~an hour a day getting ready—and ~700 leads flagged for follow‑up over three months were never touched.
After
An automated daily brief for all 33 reps—CRM + county property‑appraiser data + owner research → talking points and dos & don’ts. About an hour a day, back per rep.
Capacity and leakage—in the language a CEO actually cares about.The Storm Smart engagement
  • Recorded the top rep’s 1‑hour prep on a screen‑share
  • Turned it into a knowledge base + a reusable skill
  • Automated a daily brief that runs for every rep
  • ~700 never‑followed‑up leads surfaced by the analysis
  • ~1 hour/day of selling capacity returned, per rep
