How every engagement runs

Capture. Build. Prove. Spread.

Four steps, weeks not years, with a human in the loop the whole way, and the capability left inside your team.

1

Capture

We record an expert doing the real work and turn it into a knowledge base and a reusable skill.

2

Build

We build it into the tools your team already opens: internal apps, dashboards, automations.

3

Prove

A working tool and hours back, in weeks. Not a roadmap, not a deck. The thing itself.

4

Spread

We start with one team of 3 to 5 and turn them into champions, then keep going, training group after group until champions reach across the company.
